Ingredients

  • 1 ⅓ cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 ½ teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon light brown sugar
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 475 degrees F (245 degrees C).
  2. Once the dough has risen, turn it out onto a lightly floured surface.
  3. Roll dough into thin flat circles, about 8 inches in diameter.
  4. Cook two at a time on preheated baking sheets or a baking stone until puffed up and golden brown, about 5 minutes
  5. Repeat for remaining loaves.
